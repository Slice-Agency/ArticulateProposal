Executive Summary

The algorithm is built.
Now it needs to be found.

Articulate has a superior product. The challenge isn't the app — it's building the digital presence from scratch while activating a paid acquisition channel that actually converts.

The product

Articulate is a mobile app aimed at English speakers who want to expand their vocabulary intelligently. Unlike apps that teach from zero, Articulate targets users who are already functional in the language — offering personalized word recommendations powered by a proprietary algorithm the team is continuously improving.

The conversion model

The free trial requires a payment method at signup. When the 2-week period ends, the annual subscription activates automatically — with no additional friction for the user. That mechanism is a central conversion lever: users who complete onboarding and reach day 14 convert without being asked again. The strategy is built around maximizing that activation rate.

The core insight

The trial doesn't end with a paywall. It ends with a subscription that's already running.

  • Payment method at signup
  • Auto-charge on day 14
  • Zero friction at conversion
  • Onboarding is the product

Strategy

Three channels.
One funnel.

Each channel plays a specific role. ASO is always on. Apple Search Ads captures existing intent. Meta scales what's already working. They don't compete — they stack.

A

ASO as the permanent foundation

App Store Optimization runs from day one and never stops. Title, subtitle, description, screenshots, and keywords — all tuned for maximum organic visibility in the vocabulary / English learning niche. Every download from organic search is zero-cost acquisition.

B

Apple Search Ads as the acquisition engine

The most efficient paid channel for iOS apps in early stage. Targets users already searching for this type of app — highest intent, lowest CAC. Campaigns start in Phase 2 once the creative foundation is ready, then scale in Phase 3.

C

Meta Ads & organic as scale layer

Organic content on Instagram and TikTok builds brand, tests angles, and generates the creative intelligence that feeds paid campaigns. Meta Ads activates in Phase 3 using lookalike audiences built from the first converted users.

The logic

Don't spend on ads before the foundation is built. Every dollar spent on bad creative is a dollar that doesn't convert.

  • ASO before ads
  • Organic before paid scale
  • Apple Search Ads first
  • Meta only on validated angles
Execution · 3 phases

Build. Traction. Scale.

Each phase has a clear objective and unlocks the next. No phase is skipped — the sequence is the strategy.

Phase 1
Digital Foundations
Months 1–2

Branding & Content Curation

  • Deep niche curation: tone, language, and cultural references for native English-speaking audience
  • Identity implementation across all digital touchpoints based on the existing Brand Guideline
  • Instagram and TikTok profile setup: bio, profile photo, highlights, and first posts
  • Asset coordination: screenshots, demo videos, high-res prints from the app

ASO — App Store Optimization

  • Keyword research for the vocabulary / English learning niche on App Store
  • Title, subtitle, description, screenshots, and preview video optimization
  • Category and keyword configuration in App Store Connect

Social Media Setup

  • Brand voice definition and content pillars aligned to Articulate
  • Editorial calendar for Instagram and TikTok — minimum 3 posts per week
  • First 8–12 posts live before any paid campaign activates
Phase 2
Traction
Months 2–4

Apple Search Ads

  • Search Match and Keyword campaigns — targeting competitor keywords (Vocabulary Builder, Learn Words, etc.)
  • A/B testing of creatives (screenshots) to optimize CTR
  • Weekly bid and keyword optimization based on real conversion data
  • Budget: $1,500/month directed to Apple Search Ads

Active Organic Content

  • Consistent publishing on Instagram and TikTok — minimum 3x per week
  • Formats: vocabulary tip reels, "do you know what this means?", comparatives, testimonials
  • Competitor ad analysis to inform creative angles
Phase 3
Scale
Months 4–6

Meta Ads (Instagram / Facebook)

  • App install campaigns targeting lookalike audiences built from first converted users
  • Creatives based on the best-performing organic content
  • Retargeting users who visited the App Store listing and didn't convert

TikTok Ads — Conditional

  • Activates only if organic TikTok content shows relevant traction
  • Spark Ads format: amplify organic posts that already performed well

Free Trial → Paid Conversion Optimization

  • Funnel analysis: download → activation → day 14 → conversion
  • Onboarding and push notification recommendations to increase trial-to-paid rate
  • Pricing or incentive adjustment proposal if the data supports it
